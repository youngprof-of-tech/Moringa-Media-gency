The Life Blood Of A Business.

It's probably not a new thing to know that one of the things that keeps humans alive is the blood in our veins. it is what is actually keeping our body functioning well, and it as well keeps our brain alive. when the body seems to be running short of blood, then a person begins to die gradually, and immediately there is no blood in the body again, the person dies. life is in the blood, so it is very important that you have enough blood to regulate the functions of the body. Now this also applies to businesses, every business out there, whether big or small needs one thing to survive and that is sales.


sale is the life blood of every business...


Every business needs to make sales. It is the only way you can grow your business, and it is the only way you can get the money to keep doing what you are doing.


Sales are important because they help the business stay afloat. If there were no sales, then all of your money would go straight into the company's bank account and nothing would get done with it. Sales are what pays the bills and keeps your business running smoothly. sales is the ultimate goal of any business or company, it is wat determine growth and progress of the business. It also determines if the company's product or service is accepted by its market. where there's no sales the business begins to die and if this happens over a long time then the business would eventually die (fold up)


So many small scale business and supposedly big/large companies (businesses) mostly find themselves suffering from little or no sales at all. This sometimes leads to frustration of the business owners, employees and sales team, as there seems to be no results for their investment in the business. Any business whether large or small can suffer from low sales as there are factors responsible for low sales and if this is not tackled, no matter how big the investment is, the business is likely to go bankrupt if there's no sales. There are a lot of strategy that is responsible for crushing sales in your business or company, and this starts from before the company or business is created. Having a good sales strategy does not start when the product or service is created, it is very important that sales strategy is done along side with the business plan/model. when this is done, it's makes it easier for the the sales team to convert leads into sales.


Here are four things you need to consider if you want to have enough sales for your business.


  1. Market research: Market research is the the process of gathering information about your products, the market , the customer/clients/consumers and your competition in the market. Market research is an important part of the business world. It allows businesses to make informed decisions about their products, services, and pricing. It's helps you understand your customers and their needs, which will give you the knowledge you need to know what kind of products or services they want. You can use this information to create a product line that meets those needs, or even create a new product altogether.

    Market research can be done at any stage of your business. Nevertheless, it is very important for new start-ups. New businesses need to know that there is a potential market for their product or service before starting up the business.
    The information you gather while conducting market research for your start-up will feed into a number of sections when drafting your business plan. It enables you to predict your sales potential. identify your target market’s demographic profile, choose the right business location and set your market price. Making a market research will enable you create a good marketing plan for your business based on well informed statistics about your market. Businesses already in existence may require to conduct market research to make sure that they continue to serve their clients by keeping up with current trends, get ahead of their competition always, or discover new markets for potential growth. Market research can be helpful when existing businesses are thinking about making significant changes to their business like an expansion or relocation and sometimes, rebranding. With that being said, there are many ways to conduct market research. For example, you could use focus groups or surveys to gather opinions from potential clients or customers. You could also hire an expert to do this for you if you have the budget for it. You can also go online and look at the reviews of other people who have used certain products or services in the past. This is a brilliant way to find out if there is anything negative reviews about a product or service before buying it. The most efficient way of doing market research is by using surveys and other online tools. These will allow you to ask questions about your customers' interests, habits and behaviors, so that you can learn more about who they are and what they need from your business.
  2. Branding: This is the most important part of a business as it defines how the market and audience relates with your business. every business/company needs a brand in order to survive. A good brand is a will definitely create a good perspective and build a good reputation for your business.
    So what is Branding? Branding can Simply be referred to as an identity that serves as a voice of your business. It has to do with the overall perception of how your users/buyers perceive your business/company. Simply put, branding is what comes to the mind of your audience whenever the name of your business is mentioned. Branding is always viewed from the perspective of the customers and audience. It is the totality of the image that is registered in the mind of audience whenever they think about your business. Branding is everything about the business represents and it holds the entire image of the business, From the name, to logo and colors palette, to services, to customer service, relation and feedback make up the brand. when a business grows from being a business to a brand then the business is has a good brand.
    A branding is the overrall identity of what a business is, And the message that separate them from their competitors and how they want their potential customers to view them.
    Having a good brand is a very effective way to build a business and make sales
  3. Advertising: Advertising is a marketing communication that employs an openly sponsored, non- personal message to promote or sell a product, service or idea. Sponsors of advertising are typically businesses wishing to promote their products or services ~Wikipedia advertising is a medium whereby the which business display their products or services for their consumer to purchase. like the definition given by Wikipedia, it is a method of promoting one's good, product , ideas and services. Billboards, magazines radio advert e.t.c are all examples of offline advertisment.
