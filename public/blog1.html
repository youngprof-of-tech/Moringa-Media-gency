Why Your Business Needs Branding?

As an Entrepreneur, You’ve probably heard the statistics that 10 out of 11 business fail in the first 5 years, or the stats of many business wind up daily on Statista. sales!! The holy grail. Every business survival and challenge is dependent on them making sales or the get caught up in the storm and get liquidated in time. And this is why we are considering a huge factor determinant on success of your new startup or failure.



What’s Branding? Branding can Simply be referred to as an identity that serves as a voice of your brand. It has to do with the overall perception of how your users/buyers perceive your brand…. Branding is what separate the big dogs in the industry and the others. You are probably confused right now thinking, but I’ve a logo, and a tagline isn’t that a brand identity. Yhhh yiu are right, but branding goes way beyond the logo or just the tagline to more of psychological appealing and communication to customers using your brand. A speaking brand is a selling brand….


Now let’s in-depth into what a brand is, types and how to apply them to yours.


Welcome to the club.... In this article, I will be walking you through step by step process of crafting the perfect branding for your business.. A branding is the overall identity of what a business is, their message that separate them from their competitors and how they want their potential customers to view them. Also according to Seth Godin, brand is the set of expectations, memories, stories and relationships that, taken together, account for a consumer’s decision to choose one product or service over another.

Also Jeff bezos, CEO of amazon ha this to say about branding, ,Your brand is what other people say about you when you’re not in the room.


Branding is your identity that connects your customers to your product by appealing to their subconscious mind. Gerald Zaltman also write that 95% of customer purchasing decisions are subconscious..


Here are steps on how you craft the perfect branding for your business.


  1. Create Buyers Persona: this is the first step in branding your business. this allows you to understand your market audience and also getting informations as to what and how to sell to them. creating a buyers persona enables you to be able to assess your customers closely. buyers persona requires you conduct a market research by engaging with your prospective customers and also sending Q&A either through the traditional means or using social media, emails or any other relevant platforms where your target audience can be found. This approach can greatly help boost your business by establishing a connection with your buyers owing to the fact that the product is originally built with them in mind and they can also resonate with it.
  2. Create a brand story: this goes to the very reason and existence of your business. a brand story is what solutions your business promise to its customers and why your product is different from your competitors. this brand story is used in crafting a tagline for your business/startup. what is a TAGLINE? A tagline is short phrase or motto that carries your business promise, mission and purpose. this tagline are longlasting and often seen on your products alongside your logo. crafting a perfect tagline for your business is very important because it explains the very purpose of business and its story. here are some top companies brand tagline to lookup while crafting out the big idea for yours. DISNEYLAND; The happiest place on earth. NIKE; Just do it. AIRBNB; Belong anywhere''
  3. Create your Logo and brand color: This step will require you outsource to a a professional logo designer to design your logo for you. a logo is simply the face of the brand. and bad logo is definitely what you should avoid. Colors have meanings and your brand color should match the exact mood of your business. Entreprenuers are often tempted to choose their favorite colors as their brand color which is totally wrong.
  4. Hire a Branding Agency: The importance of branding for a business can never be over emphasized. you can save a ton of time and mental stress by hiring an agency to carry out this for you. this service can be in reasonable pay and will come with a website, logo, colors and several designs to get your brand up and standing tall in the market. you can book a free call withS Moringa Agency via this link
  5. Consistency: Now that you've got your brand fully designed. it doesnt end there. you need to come out everyday, the same way! read that again, consistency will keep your brand identity in the memory of your customers. its often advised to use same designers to keep on with the feel of your brand and if youd need to meet a freelancer designer, communicate well showing what your brand logo,colors and stories is about to help him/her in creating the designs that will complement Your brand identity.... watch out for more tips and business articles to help strengthen your business.
