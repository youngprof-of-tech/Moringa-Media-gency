5 Reasons Why SEO is Important for Startups / Small Businesses

Search Engine Optimization, popularly known as SEO, is a very powerful tool for digital marketing. Simply put, it is a tool that makes the search engine crawl, index and give your website a higher page rank. But how does SEO help a small business grow? Let us checkout:



  1. Enhanced Visibility:With the online market, there are no geographical boundaries. Even with your small business, you can reach out to the global customers or if you are a local company, you can enhance your visibility locally, with the help of SEO. More visibility means more prospects, more sales and higher profits!
  2. Become user friendly:The more easy or SEO friendly is your website, the better it is for your business. As per a recent survey, users hardly stays 10 seconds on a website; which means you have just 10 seconds to impress a user and convince him or her to buy from your website. A SEO friendly website makes your job easy. SEO techniques involve easy choice of words and hence users can easily relate to your goods and services.
  3. Become a Brand:SEO techniques involve using powerful words. These are the words which are most frequently searched by the users on the search engines. The optimum use of this kind of words will mean as soon as the user inputs any of these words on any of the search engine, the search engine will highlight those pages which contain these words. Hence if you have to grow to be a brand you have to use proper search engine techniques.
  4. Checkout new markets:Using SEO tools means you are attracting customers throughout the world. As internet has made the world a global village, your website can be visited by customers from around the world. Hence you have the opportunity to explore the global market using right SEO services Mumbai. A part of using SEO tools is right use of social media, which is another significant part of digital marketing. SEO takes the users to your twitter or Facebook page at one go, making you popular in the business world; more likes and shares, more business for you.
